About Me

I am a Front-End developer adept at implementing the full use of html, css, and javascript along with React, and nodeJS. In my previous position I was the lead developer in most of the projects I’ve worked on, working alongside with other designers, and project managers in delivering the client’s expectations. Over the years I’ve learned to improve my attention to details and be more adaptive to varying situations.

I really enjoy the whole process of developing a website/web app, starting from the conceptualization of an idea, figuring out solutions for the functionality of different features and designing the user experience for utility and efficiency. The ability of being able to manifest an idea into existence is something I truly enjoy and to be able to work in it professionally makes it even better.

Given the opportunity, I will be a desirable addition to the company and I will make it my priority to contribute all of my skills and experiences I have gained over the years in helping to maintain a successful and productive business.
